WE School For Applied Technology

WE ATS were launched as the first ICT specialized smart schools based on a partnership between MCIT and MoETE, in light of Egypt's Vision 2030 for technical education and vocational training development through bridging the gap between theoretical education and actual labour market needs to prepare a generation of highly qualified technicians who are capable of competing in local, regional and international telecommunications market by developing their skills and providing them with necessary practical experience
